Tracking the Sun

To find the position of the Sun in the sky at a point in time and place, you need to pass year, date, local time, latitude, longitude and the timezone of the location.

To track the position of the sun at the current time, you only need the latitude and the longitude. For the current date, time and the timezone the program automatically figures out that info based on your system datetime.

Tracking a Star

Similar to how you find the position of the sun at a point in time and place. Click on the toggle button on the top to switch to find the position of Stars. You need to pass the year, date, local time, latitude, longitude, timezone, Right Ascension of the Star and the Declination of the Star.

The Result will be the Altitude and the Azimuth of the Star. You can also choose to track it continuously for the current datetime of your machine.
